Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to commonly asked questions about visiting Kampong Thom.

You can reach Kampong Thom by bus from Phnom Penh (approximately 3 hours) or from Siem Reap (approximately 2.5 hours). There are regular bus services throughout the day from both cities. Private taxis are also available for more flexibility.

The best time to visit Sambor Prei Kuk is during the dry season from November to March. Early morning (before 10 AM) or late afternoon (after 3 PM) provide the most comfortable temperatures and the best lighting for photography.

Yes, Kampong Thom offers several accommodation options ranging from budget guesthouses to mid-range hotels. Most accommodations are located in Kampong Thom city, which serves as a good base for exploring the surrounding attractions.

We recommend spending at least 2-3 days in Kampong Thom to fully explore the major attractions including Sambor Prei Kuk, Santuk Mountain, and the surrounding cultural sites. If you plan to visit Tonle Sap Lake, add an extra day to your itinerary.
